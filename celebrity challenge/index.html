About Steve

Steve Harvey, born on January 17, 1957, in Welch, West Virginia, is an American comedian, actor, and television host. He began his career in stand-up comedy before achieving widespread fame with his own sitcom, "The Steve Harvey Show." He later became known for hosting various game shows, including "Family Feud" and "The Steve Harvey Show," as well as his radio program "The Steve Harvey Morning Show." Additionally, he's authored several books on relationships and self-help. Harvey is recognized for his humor, charisma, and motivational speaking.
